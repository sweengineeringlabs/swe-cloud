# Support Guide

If you need help with CloudEmu, please use the following resources:

## 1. Documentation
Before asking for help, please review the documentation:
- **[Getting Started](./doc/overview.md)**
- **[Architecture](./doc/3-design/architecture.md)**
- **[Configuration](./doc/overview.md#configuration)**

## 2. Community Support
- **GitHub Discussions**: For general questions, patterns, and architectural advice.
- **GitHub Issues**: For bug reports and specific feature requests.

## 3. Commercial Support
For enterprise support or custom service implementations, please contact the maintainers at [enterprise-support@example.com].

---

**Tip**: When reporting an issue, always include your OS, `cargo --version`, and any relevant Terraform or SDK code.
